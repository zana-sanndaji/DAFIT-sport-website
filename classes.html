Lose weight

Losing weight is a crucial aspect of our fitness philosophy at DAFIT. We understand the importance of maintaining a healthy weight and strive to train both your body and mind to achieve this goal. Our approach focuses on making your fitness journey enjoyable while ensuring you break a sweat and see results. We're proud to share that many of our clients have experienced significant fat loss, with an average of 10% reduction in just 20 days. This is a testament to the effectiveness of our programs and the dedication of our clients. By choosing DAFIT, you're not only joining a gym but also embarking on a path to a fitter and happier you. Our team is here to support and motivate you every step of the way. Get ready to shed those pounds, feel great, and embrace a healthier lifestyle with DAFIT!

Strength and Conditioning Class

Welcome to DAFIT Strength and Conditioning Class! In our classes, we prioritize more than just weight loss and fitness. We understand the importance of proper form and technique while performing challenging exercises, as well as the need to build and improve strength without risking injury. Our strength and conditioning classes are designed to help you develop excellent form, increase your overall strength, and prevent injuries. We believe that by mastering the fundamentals and focusing on quality movement, you can achieve remarkable results while minimizing the risk of harm. Our experienced trainers will guide you through a variety of exercises and movements, ensuring that you understand the proper techniques and providing personalized attention to help you reach your goals safely and effectively. So come join us at DAFIT Strength and Conditioning Class, where we'll help you build a strong, resilient body that can tackle any challenge life throws your way!

Run Club

Welcome to DAFIT Run Club! We believe that being a good runner sets the foundation for any sports or exercises you undertake. That's why we're excited to invite you to join our monthly 5k and 10k runs, happening every second Sunday of the month. Our runs are not about racing; they're about coming together as a community, having fun, and staying active. Whether you're a seasoned runner or just starting out, our runs offer something for everyone. Plus, we've got prizes and refreshments waiting for you at the finish line to celebrate your achievements! Participating in our Run Club is a great way to improve your endurance, stay motivated, and prepare yourself for the week ahead at the gym. So lace up your shoes, join us for a run, and let's have a fantastic time together!
